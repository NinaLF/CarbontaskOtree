  • Dietary behavior (based on 2000 kcal average daily requirement): The respective person either follows a meat-based diet (omnivorous) or changed to a plant-based diet (vegetarian)
  • Recycling behavior: The respective person either changed to recycling comprehensively or does not recycle
  • Regionality of food purchases: The respective person either changed tobuys and consumes mainly regional food or mainly imported food items

  • Traveling on vacation: The respective person either takes two medium distance flights a year (=3-6hours) or or changed to taking the train (e.g. London-Paris or London-Switzerland)
