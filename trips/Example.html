How to Fill in the Diary:


  1. Start and End Locations:
    • Please first note where the car was parked at the beginning and at the end of the day.
    • If the car was not used on a day, select 'Home' as both the start and end locations. Alternatively, select the specific location where the car was parked for the entire day.

  2. Details for Each Trip:
    • Destination: Where you went
    • Start Time: When you started the trip (use 24-hour format, e.g., 5:30 PM is 17:30)
    • Distance: How many miles you drove
    • Duration: How long the trip took in minutes

Example
Please carefully study this example. Here the car starts at home. Then, they drive to work, stay there until 15:30, and drive back home. In the evening, their partner picks up the kids from school with the same electric car and returns home. At the end of the day the car is at home.
