Instructions: How to fill in your car's driving diary


Please now again think about last year in August. On the next pages, write down all the trips made with your electric car each day of the week. This includes trips by you, your partner, children, friends, or anyone else using your electric car.

  • What Counts as a Trip?
    • A trip is driving your electric car to a place and parking it for at least 20 minutes.
    • Examples: Going to work, school, grocery store, or any other location, including returning home.
    • Only include trips with your electric car.

How to Fill in the Diary:

  1. Start and End Locations:
    • For each day, first note where your car starts (e.g., home) and where it ends (e.g., home).
    • If the car was not used, choose "Home" for both start and end locations.

  2. Details for Each Trip:
    • Destination: Where you went.
    • Start Time: When you started the trip (use 24-hour format, e.g., 5:30 pm is 17:30).
    • Distance: How many miles you drove.
    • Duration: How long the trip took in minutes.
