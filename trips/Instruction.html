Instructions for the Next Pages:


On the following pages, please document all the trips made by your electric car over the course of each day of the week. This includes trips made by you, your partner, children, friends, or anyone else using your electric car.

Important:

  • A trip is defined as driving your electric car to a destination and parking it there for at least 20 minutes. A destination can be work, school, groceries, or any other place, including home. This means you should record trips both from home to other destinations and from other destinations back to home.
  • Only include trips made with your electric car.
  • Do not include trips taken with any other vehicles.

For each day, please first indicate where your car started the day (e.g., at home) and where the car was parked at the end of the day. If the car was not used on a particular day, please select "Home" as both the starting and ending location.

For each trip of the day, please provide the following information:
  • Destination: Where you go
  • Start Time: When you start the trip (in 24h format: e.g. 5:30pm is 17:30)
  • Distance: How many miles you drive
  • Duration: How long the trip takes in minutes
Example
Here is an example of what your trips for one day might look like:

You start your day at home. Then, you drive to work, stay there until 14:30, and drive back home. In the evening, your partner picks up the kids from school with the same electric car and returns home. The car ends the day at home.
